About Us

ようこそ | Discover Konpeki

Konpeki Plaza - an architectural marvel created solely for the corporate elite. Renowned for our combination of American largesse and Japanese minimalism, every square inch caters to luxury tastes and comes packed with the most decadent of entertainments, with just a hint of mystery to keep things exciting.


Our buildings feature 80 floors, 420 rooms, 20 conference halls, and a top floor penthouse. Guests have 24-hour-access to the bar, an art gallery, sauna, pool, gym, massage parlor, braindance parlor, and more!

Lapis Lazuli Suite - A luxury suite with one double bed, a television screen, a seating area, and one bathroom.

One of our private lounges, complete with televisions, minibars, and complimentary attendant services.


The wonderful Konpeki Bar and Restaurant. Fully stocked with the best liquor money can buy.
